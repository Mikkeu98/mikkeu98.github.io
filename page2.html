lonely

A page in my zine.

I wanted to convey the feeling of being surrounded by others but still feel alone. While with other people, time moves too quickly. The spheres give a suffocating feeling, surrounding the page.

Sometimes in relationships, you want to be with a person all the time. Even when you spend hours with them you don’t want to say goodbye.

You have to but it’s so lonely without them. That could also be another meaning, you can interpret it a few ways.

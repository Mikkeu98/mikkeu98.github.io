STELLAR.

A double page spread in the zine I am working on.

I wanted the circle to appear more organic, so the circle has shades of green, yellow, and blue in it.

I wanted it to almost look like a planet shrouded in clouds.

I was going for a modern, almost futuristic look so I wanted the smooth, shaded objects to be under these flat drips of color.

This whole spread was full of hyper-saturated colors so the way I balanced it was to put it on a white blank background.

The words match the drips to have some unity, but words with emphasis are just left black.

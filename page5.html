Laugh Over it: With a big heart

Another spread in my zine.

I wanted a psychedelic feel with this piece. To bring it all together the center is a contrasting color and unaltered. I think laughter is important to any friendship or relationship, being able to laugh about things and not be too serious.

I used illustrator to make the distortion. At first I used the layer effects on the whole page but it was too distracting so I limited it to the part the eye is supposed to look at.

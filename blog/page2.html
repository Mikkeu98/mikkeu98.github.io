Roboto Typeface Research

26 April 2018

Roboto was designed by Christian Robertson. Robertson is an interface designer for Google. It was released in 2011 but redesigned in 2014. It is in the category of Sans-Serif and classified as Neo-grotesque.It is the standard font for phones operating on Android. It is mainly used for smaller text or body text.

Roboto is being used on websites like https://info-buddhism.com , https://medium.com , and http://www.dosed.co.uk . It is also used in packaging, like in BLK water, and posters, brochures, and books.

Yes, the font is used properly. It is being used for the body text and other information. It is used for more important details besides the title or header.
